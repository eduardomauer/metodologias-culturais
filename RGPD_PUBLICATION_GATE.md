# RGPD Publication Gate

No public file may include personal data, identifiable contributors, sensitive narratives, faces, contact details, private emails, location-linked personal testimony or unvalidated consent.

## Blocked by default

- raw testimonies;
- private images;
- unlicensed photographs;
- faces or identifiable persons;
- contact data;
- minors' data;
- sensitive social, health, political, religious or identity data;
- internal inference methods;
- private OCSR or Atlas operational notes.

## Required before publication

- minimisation;
- purpose definition;
- consent or lawful basis;
- anonymisation or pseudonymisation when needed;
- access layer;
- review status;
- withdrawal route when applicable.

Status: gate active. Formal legal review still required before any claim of full compliance.
