# Model Training and Dataset Use Prohibited

No material in this repository may be used for model training, fine-tuning, embeddings or vector databases for commercial reuse, scraping, datasetisation, image generation prompts designed to imitate style, reconstruction of visual systems, aesthetic derivation or automated extraction of protected curatorial methods.

This applies to text, images, assets, characters, diagrams, documentation, datasets and metadata unless a file-specific licence explicitly authorises otherwise.

Any exception requires written authorisation from the rights holder.
