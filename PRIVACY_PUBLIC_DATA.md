# Privacy and Public Data Policy

Public repositories must not contain personal data unless publication has a documented lawful basis, explicit scope, minimisation, retention logic and validation.

Narratives, community contributions, cultural records and territorial materials must be reviewed before publication.

Where possible, public material should use aggregated, anonymised, non-identifying or public-source records with clear provenance and access-level classification.

The public layer must remain separate from restricted, confidential and internal methodological layers.

## Release criteria

Before a dataset, document or interface is made public, the following must be checked:

- source and provenance;
- rights and licence;
- consent where applicable;
- personal data minimisation;
- access level;
- validation status;
- responsible entity;
- version and date.
