# PT/EU Legal and Compliance Gate

This repository is a release candidate and must not be described as fully compliant until formal review is complete.

## Required legal checks

- Portuguese association governance and institutional responsibility.
- Copyright and neighbouring rights.
- Image, voice and personality rights.
- RGPD and Portuguese data protection law.
- Cultural heritage and intangible heritage safeguards.
- Open data and reuse terms for public-sector sources.
- Accessibility and plain-language requirements.
- Security and secret-management review.
- Funding documentation traceability.

## European alignment

- RGPD.
- AI Act when AI-related components are later deployed.
- NIS2 only if operational scope makes it applicable.
- Interoperable Europe principles.
- Open data and FAIR/CARE metadata practice.
- Creative Europe, Erasmus+ and UNESCO alignment only as programme-readiness, never as funding guarantee.

Status: active gate, not a legal opinion.
