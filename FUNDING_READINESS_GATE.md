# Funding Readiness Gate

This repository may support future applications. It is not a funding guarantee.

## Minimum evidence for programme use

- project summary;
- legal entity;
- responsible person;
- target programme;
- partners and roles;
- budget basis;
- schedule;
- outputs;
- indicators;
- public communication evidence;
- RGPD and rights review;
- accessibility plan;
- sustainability plan;
- repository, DOI and archive identifiers when available.

## Compatible framing areas

- cultural mediation;
- intangible heritage;
- digital cultural infrastructure;
- accessibility;
- participation;
- open knowledge;
- non-formal learning;
- territorial cohesion;
- European interoperability.

Status: active gate pending project-specific eligibility review.
