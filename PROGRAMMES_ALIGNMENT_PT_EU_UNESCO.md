# Programme Alignment Note

This repository may support documentation for public-interest cultural, educational, scientific, accessibility, participation, heritage and interoperability actions.

## Alignment areas

- Portugal public funding and technical operation files.
- European open data and interoperability frameworks.
- Erasmus+ cultural learning, cooperation, inclusion and participation priorities.
- Creative Europe cultural cooperation and audience development priorities.
- UNESCO cultural heritage and intangible cultural heritage principles.
- FAIR and CARE-aligned metadata governance.

## Rule

No programme alignment statement is a funding guarantee. Each application must include its own eligibility check, budget, partners, indicators, target group, outputs, schedule and evidence.
